What is Market Basket Analysis?

Market basket analysis is a data mining technique that identifies the patterns of items that customers buy together. It can help retailers to optimize their product placement, promotions, and cross-selling strategies.

Programming Language(s) Used:
Python

Modules Used:
Pandas
apriori(mlxtend)
association rules(mlxtend)

How I carried out the Project

Step 1:Obtain Data
First, I obtained the data needed and loaded it into a pandas dataframe.
Step 2:Data Cleaning and Pre-Processing
Cleaned the data and transformed it by encoding it into a one-hot matrix
Step 3: Analysis
First, I applied the apriori algorithm from the mlxtend library to generate frequent itemsets that meet a minimum support threshold. I then applied the association_rules function from the mlxtend library to generate association rules that meet a minimum confidence and lift threshold.
Step 4: Conclude Analysis
I analysed and interpereted the results of the analysis. Here I took note of the most interesting and actionable insights from the data judging by the relevant metrics (lift,support,leverage and conviction). These can then be used in business to make changes to how shelf space is being used in the supermarket for example. It can also be used to determine the right products to recommend online when a customer adds a certain product to the cart. The results from this analysis are powerful in that they are useful in bringing out extra sales for the business
