What is Customer Segmentation?

Customer segmentation analysis is a method of dividing customers into groups or clusters based on common characteristics. This can help businesses understand their customers better, tailor their marketing strategies, and optimize their resources.

How I carried out the Project

Step 1:ETL
I began by loading in the data from a website and exploring it. The data was clean but it had extra columns that I did not need for the analysis. I removed these columns.
Step 2:Data Pre-Processing
Usually there is need for a RFM table to be created from the data when conducting this kind of analysis but this time, there was not enough data to do that, and the data had already been processed to capture some of the featuers of a RFM table. The data I had to my disposal was for A customer's annual income and an arbitrary spending score, which were enough to conduct this analysis.
Step 3: Determining Optimal number of Clusters
I used Within Clusters Sum of Squares to do this. I wrote a for loop that conducted K-Means clustering for k =1 to k=10. The loop also saved the inertia, which is useful in determining optimal number of lags.A graph was useful to show that 5 was the optimal number of clusters. At this point, we knew our customers could be divided into 5 different groups given the data we had on them.
Step 4: Model Training
Next, I trained a k-means clustering machine learning algorithm to now learn and predict which of the 5 groups every customer belonged in.
Step 5: Visualize the Findings
